Kcal — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Data Collection

This app collects no personal data. All information you enter — including food logs, calorie targets, weight, height, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using local SQLite storage. No data is ever transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

Accounts

This app requires no account, email address, or login of any kind.

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time via Settings → Data & Privacy → Clear All Local Data. This action is irreversible and immediately removes all data from your device.

Analytics

This app contains no analytics SDK, crash reporting service, or behavioral tracking of any kind.

Permissions

The app may request camera access for barcode scanning and notification permission for optional meal reminders. These permissions are requested only when you initiate those features and are never used for data collection.
