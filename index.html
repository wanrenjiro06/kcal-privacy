Privacy Policy

by RidgeField Apps  ·  Last updated: June 5, 2026

Your data never leaves your device
🔒

No Data Collection

Kcal collects zero personal data. Everything you enter — food logs, calorie targets, weight, height, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using local SQLite storage. Nothing is ever transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

👤

No Account Required

Kcal requires no account, email address, sign-in, or registration of any kind. You can use the full app without identifying yourself.

🗑️

Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all locally stored data at any time via Settings → Data & Privacy → Clear All Local Data. This action is immediate and irreversible.

📊

No Analytics or Tracking

Kcal contains no analytics SDK, crash reporting service, ad network, or behavioral tracking of any kind. We do not know how you use the app.

📷

Permissions

The app may request camera access for barcode scanning and notification permission for optional meal reminders. These are requested only when you use those features and are never used for data collection or advertising.

✉️

Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach us at ridgefieldapps@gmail.com
